Research Question: How do spatial factors and proximity to flooding affect reductions in oil production following flood events in Poza Rica? 


Research Type: Predictive Analysis 


Analysis Plan: 


I. Define Research Question 

II. Process & Analyze Data 

  Clean data and make it tidy 
  
  What is the data behavior? 
  
III. Identify Sources of Bias 

IV. Develop Optimal Research Design 

  What model does the data behavior suggest? 
  
  What are potential sources of omitted variable basis? 
  
  What are the assumptions of our selected model? 
  
V. Implement Research Design 

  What are the model diagnostics? 
  
VI. Re-evaluate Research Design 

  Based on model diagnostics, are there areas for improvement? 
  
  Was there overfitting or other issues?
  
  How are the errors?
  
VII. Interpret & Analysis Results 

  What do the results mean in context?
  
  Are these results surprising or in line with expectations? 

  
VIII. Write Final Report 

  Describes the research question, its context, and its importance 
  
  Describes methods utilized and specific choices 
  
  Presents results
